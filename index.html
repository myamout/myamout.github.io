Getting There

Kahului Airport

1 Keolani Pl, Kahului, HI 96732, USA

West Maui and the Ritz-Carlton is located about 35 miles (approximately 60 minutes by car) from the airport. Due to the distance and island time drivers we recommend staying in West Maui, specifically Kaanapali or Kapalua, if possible to be close to the festivities.

Navigating Maui

Rental cars are the easiest way to get around Maui. Uber, private drivers, and taxis are also available on the island. If you are a guest at the Ritz-Carlton, Kapalua Resort shuttle is available for transportation to nearby beaches, restaurants, shopping and golf courses within Kapalua Resort.

FAQ

Attire

The wedding is a beach-formal event. The ceremony will take place on the lawn overlooking the ocean. Men will be in slacks and a button down or a suit. Ties are optional. Shorts, Hawaiian shirts, flip-flops are best suited for the Aloha party. As the ceremony will be on grass, we recommend that women may want to opt for a thicker heel or flat.

Traveling with Others

While we know many of you will be brining your families and loved ones with you to Maui, we ask that only those addressed on the formal invitation be in attendance at all wedding events.
